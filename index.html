IOT Based Suicide Prevention System:

The IoT-Based Suicide Prevention System is a smart safety solution designed to detect and prevent suicide attempts in real-time. It uses sensors like IR or load sensors placed on ceiling fans to identify unusual activity, such as the presence of a rope. When triggered, the system sends immediate alerts to caregivers or emergency contacts through IoT communication, enabling timely intervention.

Medicine Reminder App:

The Medicine Reminder App is a digital solution designed to help patients take their medicines on time by sending timely notifications and alerts. It allows users to track their daily dosage, mark medicines as taken, and maintain consistent medication routines. Caretakers can also monitor patient adherence and receive low stock alerts for timely refills.

AI based Real Time Sign Language Translator:

The AI-Based Real-Time Sign Language Translator is an innovative solution designed to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and speech-impaired community and others. It uses computer vision and deep learning techniques to detect hand gestures in real time and convert them into text, speech, or visual outputs. The system also supports voice-to-sign translation, making communication two-way. This project aims to promote inclusivity, improve accessibility, and enable smoother interaction in daily life through a smart and user-friendly interface.
